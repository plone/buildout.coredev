-------------------------------------------
New features:

- Add ``dos_protection`` config.

  To increase this limit, you can add this in your instance recipe, and choose your own limit::

    zope-conf-additional =
      <dos_protection>
        form-memory-limit 4MB
      </dos_protection>

  [@mamico] (#191)
